D3 - Neo4j

A demos app using Neo4j to diplay recipe data using d3.js

Ingredients

(Small light green nodes)

Doubleclick - Filters data to a selection of recipes that contain that ingredient (and the ingredients of those recipes). If there are more than 10 that match a random selection of 10 recipes are returned.

Recipes

(Larger dark green nodes)

Click - Click and drag to fix the recipe nodes in position.

Doubleclick - Doubleclick to release a fixed recipe node.

Selected

(Yellow node)

The yellow node is the current ingredient node that the graph is centered around. That ingredient appears in all the recipes in the graph.
